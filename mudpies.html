Mud Pies

Dazzling Delicious Homemade Mud Pie

Who'da thunk delicious could be so damn simple? For the treat of a lifetime, look no further.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of White Bread
  • 1 jar of Creamy Peanut Butter
  • 1 tsp of Cinnamon Sugar
  • 1 tsp Honey

Directions

  1. Toast two slices of bread
  2. Slab generous amounts of peanut butter on both sides of the bread
  3. Sprinkle the cinnamon sugar evenly between the two slices, suga
  4. Spread the honey evenly between the two slices, hunny
  5. Cut into 1 inch slices so theres enough for everyone
