Bails Chilli

Bail's Hot'N'Spicy Bathroom Blowout Breeze Blowin Chilli

Hot and fresh y'all, come and get it. Old Bails Chilli is ready and you better be...might be wise to drink half a bottle of pepto before feastin on this... unless you enjoy peeing fire out your anus

Ingredients

  • 1 tbps chilli powder
  • 1/2 tbsp cumin
  • 1/2 tbsp oregeno
  • 1/2 tbsp onion powder
  • 1/2 tbsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp smoked peprika
  • 1 tsp cayan
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp pepper
  • 14oz can of fiery red tomatoes
  • 14oz can of green chillis
  • 1 can of pinto beans
  • 1 can of navy beans
  • 2lbs ground beef
  • 1 yellow onion
  • 1 tbsp minced garlic
  • 1 zucchini chopped into half moons
  • 1 can of corn
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • Sour Cream
  • Shredded Cheese

Directions

  1. Prepare all spices in a small mixing bown
  2. Chop all veggies
  3. Toss all of the veggies into the instant pot with some olive oil and saute for 5-7 minutes on medium
  4. Toss in the ground beef and garlic, cook until brown
  5. Turn off the saute and put the chicken broth and the spices in the pot
  6. Set to high pressure for 15 minutes--nows a good time to drink that pepto bismal
  7. When finished, quick release
  8. Serve with a dollup of sour cream and sprinkle with cheese to your liking and enjoy. If you do not have pepto handy, it is wise to stay near a bathroom.
